Multi-component peptide complex HairTIDE PLUS – is a modern and reliable way to healthy and perfect hair and nails.

Package volume - 30 capsules.

Intended use (prevention and as a part of complex treatment):

  • hair loss, dry hair, fragile nails, nail dystrophy;
  • hair injuries of different nature (nutritional disorders and diet, lack of vitamins and minerals in the body; the negative impact of the external environment; medications; hormonal disorders, stress and tension for an extended period);
  • age-related changes in the condition of the hair and nails.

How to use: adults -1 capsule a day for 1 month, 20-30 minutes after a meal with a sufficient quantity of water. More prolonged use of the supplement is allowed from 2 to 3 months.

Contraindications: individual hypersensitivity to the supplement, during pregnancy and breastfeeding, underage children.

Net quantity: 19.32 g. (30 capsules of 0.644 g. each).

Ingredients:  BCAA amino acid complex (peptide complex NY, L-arginine,  L-proline), L-ascorbic acid (Vitamin C),  common horsetail (Equisetum Arvense L.) herb dry extract , zinc gluconate, DL-alpha-tocopheryl acetate (vitamin E), L-Selenium methionine , retinyl  acetate  (vitamin A), anti-caking agent microcrystalline cellulose,  anti-caking agent  silicon dioxide, anti-caking agent magnesium salts of fatty acids, capsule shell (hypromellose), colour  titanium dioxide.
HairTIDE PLUS is a source of zinc, selenium, vitamin C, vitamin E, vitamin A
Zinc, selenium help maintain normal hair; zinc, selenium help maintain normal conditions of nails; vitamin A plays an essential role in a cell specialization; vitamin C, vitamin E help protect cells from oxidative damage.
