The components of the complex:

Pumpkin (lat.Cucurbita pepo) seeds extract - Due to cucurbitin compounds, which are found in pumpkin seeds, pumpkin seeds are an effective anthelmintic means, as confirmed by numerous studies. Pumpkin seeds extract is rich in biologically active substances and in addition to its anti-parasitic action, provides antiulcer, hepatoprotective, bile-expelling effects; it reduces proliferation of prostate cells; has an anti-inflammatory effect, especially in diseases of the prostate and urinary system.

Papaya (lat.Carica papaya) leaves extract - The healing properties of papaya leaves have been known for a long time and have been widely used in Ayurvedic and traditional medicine. Papaya leaves have great therapeutic potential in ulcers and fungal skin diseases.  Results of in-vitro studies have confirmed the presence of anthelmintic compounds in the chemical composition of plants. Rich in components, papaya leaves help in strengthening the immune system, relieving the inflammatory processes, restoring the endocrine system, preventing tumors of various etiologies, including cancer of the cervix, breast, liver, lung, and pancreas. Enzyme papain, which contributes to digestion and assimilation of protein, is included in a pharmaceutical composition of medicines for digestion improving.

Ginger (lat.Zingiber officinale) root extract is a powerful natural immunomodulator and antiseptic. The plant has anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, antimicrobial, antioxidant, and bile-expelling properties.  In-vivo and in-vitro studies showed with a 100% result that ginger root extract represses the survival and breeding of parasites. Bitter enzymes that are found in the root create a depressing effect on the nervous system of the parasites; suppress their activity; make it impossible for parasites to breed; inhibit the growth of bacteria; have a paralyzing impact on larvae of helminths. Ginger root is effective against a wide range of parasites.

Garlic (lat.Allium sativum) root extract - has antimicrobial and antibacterial effect on Gram-positive and Gram-negative bacteria, protozoa, viruses, helminths. Garlic provides an anti-parasitic effect due to phytoncides secreted by the plant - biologically active compounds that possess strong antibiotic activity killing and suppressing the growth and proliferation of bacteria and parasites. Phytoncides of a garlic inhibit the activity of some tumor enzymes, protect cells from free radical and heavy metals damage. Garlic increases resistance to colds and other infectious diseases; it regulates the processes of skin regeneration, cleanse skin pores and maintains normalization of the intestinal microflora.

Pineapple (lat.Ananas comosus) fruit extract — The antiparasitic effect of pineapple extract in a high concentration is provided due to the content of bromelain in it — a substance with high biological activity and able to cleanse the body of parasites and their metabolic products quickly.

Wormwood (lat.Artemisia absinthium) aerial part extract has a significant antibacterial effect against most foodborne pathogens; acts as an important antimicrobial agent against many strains of gram-negative and gram-positive bacteria; has the ability to inhibit their growth. At 100% concentration it gives a good effect compared with Albendazole medication. High antioxidant activity of the wormwood extract is provided due to the high content of flavonoids.

Smilax (lat.Sarsaparilla) root extract - widely used in medicine and has anti-inflammatory, disinfectant, diuretic, bile-expelling, blood purifying properties; it gently removes toxins from a body and accelerates the healing process. The roots of Sarsaparila contain saponins, which synergetic effect help the body absorb the other medications more efficiently. Blood purifying properties of the plant make it indispensable when taking severe pain medication, radiation, and chemotherapy. In traditional medicine, it is used to treat leprosy, tumors, cancer, psoriasis and rheumatism.

Rusty Elm (lat.Ulmus fulva) bark extract - The bark of the tree is rich in chemical composition and has a strong antibacterial, diuretic, astringent, pain relieving, restorative, expectorant, healing and softening action.
